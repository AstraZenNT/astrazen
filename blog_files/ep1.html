BLOG | Episode 0

*KEYS: Too Dense. Rap = Poetry, Music

I was intending for this to be the official first episode but I ran out of time for a week of deadline, so I just had to abstract things instead that at least I have the essense of it. There might be quite a lot of information in just 6 pages that seems like it's a dense info dump…

The main inspiration from Episode 0 was the hypothetical of: "What if we freely chose to exist and suffer than to be nonexistent at all?" – I'll also use this for the next episode.

Somehow I got interested in rap after completing that week. Maybe it's a way of making the message repeatable/catchy in certain occassions that it'd feel right to listen to. Also that rap is kinda like an informal or more casual way of poetry/lyrics where one doesn't need to be that good of singing as long as they got the foundations - which is the rhythm. With that, I got interested in making my own beats from scratch mainly using my voice on sampling and creating loops. Since I didn't have music in the video version, that got me more compelled to make my own instead so I can use it without worries of copyright from using someone else's. It'd be quite nice to have someone collaborate on creating music someday though.

That's what I'm planning to integrate on the next projects so I'll have all the major components in Literature; Prose Poetry & Drama.
